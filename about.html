About HeartCare

Revolutionizing heart disease detection through artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our Mission

HeartCare was founded with a simple goal: to reduce heart disease mortality rates by enabling early detection through accessible technology. We believe that everyone deserves access to advanced health risk assessment tools, regardless of their location or economic status.

Our Technology

HeartCare utilizes a sophisticated ensemble of machine learning models trained on thousands of patient records from leading medical institutions. Our algorithms analyze multiple risk factors including:

  • Cholesterol levels
  • Blood pressure levels
  • Heart Activity
  • Chest Pain Types
  • ECG and other diagnostic data
